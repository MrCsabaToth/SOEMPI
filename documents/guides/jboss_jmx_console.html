Checking deployment in JBoss JMX console

Let's check if things are OK in JBoss's JMX console.
  1. Open up a browser and navigate to the 8080 port of the IP address what you configured as a binding address for JBoss. By default it's "http://localhost:8080". Click on "JMX Console" link.
  2. Click on the "service=JNDIView" link.
  3. Invoke the list function by clicking on it's Invoke button.
